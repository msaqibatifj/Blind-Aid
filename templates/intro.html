Blind Aid

Open App

Blind Aid is a small vision-assist app that runs an object detector (YOLOv8) on a camera feed and exposes live detection stats via a web UI.

What it does

  • Detects people, animals and common objects in the camera view.
  • Draws bounding boxes on the live video window.
  • Exposes simple realtime stats (frames processed, last detection count) to this website.
